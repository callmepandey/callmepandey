Fibonacci Series

Explanation

The Fibonacci series is a sequence of numbers in which each number (Fibonacci number) is the sum of the two preceding ones. The series starts with 0 and 1. Mathematically, it can be represented as:

F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)

Here are the first few numbers in the Fibonacci series:

  • F(0) = 0
  • F(1) = 1
  • F(2) = 1
  • F(3) = 2
  • F(4) = 3
  • F(5) = 5
